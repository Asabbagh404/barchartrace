# barchartrace
Bar chart race online editor

input data should be in a csv file.
Dates should be 'YYYY-MM-DD'

Option 1 : 

Date | Name1 | Name2
--- | --- | ---
2018-01-01 | 1 | 1
2018-02-01 | 2 | 3
2018-03-01 | 4| 7

Option 2 : 
Date | Name | Value
--- | --- | ---
2018-01-01 | Name1 | 1
2018-01-01 | Name2 | 3
2018-02-01 | Name1| 2
2018-02-01 | Name2 | 3
2018-03-01 | Name1 | 4
2018-03-01 | Name2 | 7
